Prepared an archival finding aid for the ephemera and manuscript materials in the collection.

  • Reference Librarian, Special Collections and Archives, California State Univ. Northridge (August 2006-November 2007)

  • Cataloguing Intern, Lilly Library, Indiana University (May 2006-August 2006)
